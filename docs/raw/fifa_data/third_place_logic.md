# FIFA 2026 World Cup Third-Place Qualification Logic

This document outlines the official rules used to determine which 8 of the 12 third-place teams advance to the Round of 32 in the 48-team tournament format.

## 1. Selection Criteria
Once all group stage matches are completed, the third-place teams from each of the 12 groups are ranked from 1st to 12th. The top 8 teams in this ranking advance to the Round of 32.

The comparison criteria applied to the 12 third-placed teams are, in order:
1. **Points:** Greatest number of points obtained in all group matches.
2. **Goal Difference:** Goal difference in all group matches.
3. **Goals Scored:** Greatest number of goals scored in all group matches.
4. **Wins:** Greatest number of matches won in all group matches. (Note: FIFA regulations use wins as a tiebreaker for cross-group comparisons).
5. **Fair Play Conduct:** The team with the highest fair play score (fewest deduction points) based on yellow and red cards received by players/team officials in all group matches:
   - **Yellow Card:** -1 point
   - **Indirect Red Card (second yellow card):** -3 points
   - **Direct Red Card:** -4 points
   - **Yellow Card and Direct Red Card:** -5 points
   *Note: Only one of the above deductions is applied to a single player in a single match.*
6. **Drawing of Lots:** Drawing of lots by the FIFA Organizing Committee.
